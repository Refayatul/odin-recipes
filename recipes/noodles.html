Noodles

Description

What is Noddles?

Noodles is the most loved instant noodles in Bangladesh which has won the hearts of Bangladeshi families over the decades.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • onion, garlic and chili
  • Eggs
  • water
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  • Prepare the Noodles: Check the instructions on your noodles; typically, rice noodles cook in boiling water for about 3-5 minutes. Once cooked, drain the noodles and set aside.
  • Fry the Onion, Garlic, and Chili: In a couple of tablespoons of oil, start by frying the onion on its own. Cook for a few minutes until softened, then add the garlic and chili, frying for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  • Add the Spices: Stir in the ground turmeric and curry powder into the vegetables, along with the final tablespoon of oil.
  • Scramble the Eggs: Crack the eggs directly into the pan and use a wooden spoon or silicone spatula to scramble the eggs into the fried vegetables.
  • Mix in the Noodles and Fresh Herbs: Add the cooked noodles, salt, fresh herbs, and lemon juice to the pan. Mix everything together until fully combined.
  • Season and Serve: Adjust the seasoning to taste, adding more salt, citrus, or herbs as desired. Serve immediately.
